Features

Everything your shop needs

A complete solution built specifically for battery shops.

Sales Management

Record every sale with product, customer, quantity, discount, and payment method (Cash, EasyPaisa, JazzCash, Bank Transfer). Generate professional receipts instantly and email them to customers.

Inventory & Stock Tracking

Track stock levels in real time. Get low-stock alerts when products fall below your threshold. Bulk upload products via text file. Organize by categories.

Warranty & Returns

Track warranty expiry per sale. Process warranty returns with replacement — stock automatically updated. If replacement is more expensive, due amount is shown and added to customer balance.

Customer Management

Full customer profiles with purchase history, credit balance, and warranty status. Track which customers owe money and how much.

Reports & Analytics

Revenue, profit, units sold, best sellers, monthly trends — all in one place. Export full sales report to PDF with one click.

Auto Updates

App checks for new versions automatically. When an update is available, a notification appears — click to download the latest installer from this website.

Customizable Settings

Set your shop name, phone, address, manager name. Toggle features on/off — warranty, signatures, discount, credit, reports, returns, categories.

PDF Invoice & Receipt

Professional receipt with shop branding, warranty info, signatures, and payment details. Print or email directly to customer.
